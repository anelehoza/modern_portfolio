ADVANCED SURFACE CORRECTION

Paint Correction

Paint correction removes various visual defects such
as scratching, swirling, scuffs, holograms and marring.
The removal of these defects restores the shine and clarity of your paintwork

Carbon Correction

Carbon surfaces are polished through multiple stages to remove
swirls, marring and scratches. The removal of these defects restores the shine and
clarity of your Carbon trim.

Plastic Correction

Plastic lenses are delicately sanded and polished through multiple stages to
remove oxidation & scratches. The removal of these defects
restores the shine and clarity of your Headlights & Taillights. Increasing visibility and overall cosmetics of the vehicle.

CERAMIC COATING

Exterior Coatings

Ceramic coating is a chemical polymer solution that is applied to the
exterior of a vehicle to protect it from external
paint damage. The coating ,bonds with the paint of your car and creates
an additional hydrophobic layer of protection. Benefits include:

  • - Protection against chemical stains
  • - UV protection
  • - Easy Cleaning
  • - Mirror like gloss

Interior Coatings

Ceramic coatings applied to the vehicles interior surfaces offers a variety of benefits:

  • - Long lasting protection over normal surface dressings
  • - Repels liquids ( Hydrophobic)
  • -UV protection
  • -Easy Cleaning
  • - Restore OEM finish
